RESIDENT EVIL
GAMEPLAY

The Resident Evil Franchise is a survival horror game where the player controls various protagonists. Each game will focus on the characters' stories and their fight against the villains. You`ll be faced with bioweapon's such as zombies, mold, mutated humans (Tyrant, Lickers, the 4 Lords,…). The first 6 games are played in a third person view until it changed in a 1st person view of the seventh game, and carried to the last one (Village).

MAIN CHARACTERS

The characters vary from game to game in the Resident Evil franchise. In one game you`ll play as Leon and Claire (RE2) a young, newly officer and the sister of the first male protagonist Chris, trying to escape the zombies and the infamous Mr. X and in another you`ll play as just one protagonist, Ethan Winter (in both RE 7 and RE8), a man trying to save is wife and daughter from the hands of mutated humans. There is much more character to find through the RE franchise.
